Although much of the recent research on QA has focused on improving reading com‐prehension models, in practice it doesn’t matter how good your reader is if the retriever can’t find the relevant documents in the first place! In particular, the retriever sets an upper bound on the performance of the whole QA system, so it’s important to make sure it’s doing a good job. With this in mind, let’s start by intro‐ducing some common metrics to evaluate the retriever so that we can compare the performance of sparse and dense representations.
